White Water Rafting Adventures

The purpose of a white water rafting company is to provide safe, thrilling outdoor adventure experiences on fast-flowing rivers. It aims to promote teamwork, recreation, and appreciation of nature while ensuring customer safety through trained guides and proper equipment.

History

White water rafting began as a means of river exploration in the 19th century, when explorers used wooden boats to navigate challenging waterways.

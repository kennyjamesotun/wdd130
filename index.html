Lagos

Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria and one of the most populous urban areas in Africa. It is the country’s main economic and commercial hub, known for its busy ports, financial institutions, technology startups, and entertainment industry. Located along the Atlantic coast, Lagos consists of a mix of islands and mainland districts connected by bridges. The city is famous for its vibrant culture, diverse population, energetic markets, music scene, and fast-paced lifestyle. Despite challenges such as traffic congestion and rapid urbanization, Lagos remains the center of business, innovation, and cultural influence in Nigeria.
